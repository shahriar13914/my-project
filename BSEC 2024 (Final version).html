Eastern University Business & Social Entrepreneurship club is the most active club of the university with the goal to offer a sophisticated and vibrant nexus for students, organizations and businesses of all kinds to meet, share ideas and discuss the issues of the day. Eastern University Business & Social Entrepreneurship Club is for anyone involved in the business and academic arenas.

BSEC's Goals & Activities

Goals:

  • To develop practical business know-how.
  • To enhance communication skills.
  • To develop entrepreneurship, leadership and managerial skills.
  • To develop presentation skills.
  • To enhance case solving capability.
  • To create potential career paths.
  • To develop team spirit.
  • To be on track with contemporary business events.
  • To increase networks.

Activities:

  • Business Idea/ Plan competition.
  • Social Business Idea/ Plan competition.
  • Entrepreneurship Fair.
  • Case competition.
  • Quiz, Debate competition.
  • Business trips, Exhibitions, Job Fairs.
  • Seminars, Workshops, Business program and more.
